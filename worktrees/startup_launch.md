# Startup Launch Workflow

## Goal
Turn an idea into a practical launch plan with MVP, positioning, monetization, and growth.

## Step 1 — Define Problem and Market
Skills:
- `product_strategist`
- `startup_operator`

Expected output:
- target customer
- pain points
- competing alternatives
- market opportunity
- assumptions to validate

## Step 2 — Define MVP
Skills:
- `product_strategist`
- `full_stack_engineer`

Expected output:
- MVP scope
- must-have features
- out-of-scope features
- fastest path to usable product

## Step 3 — Go-To-Market
Skills:
- `seo_marketing_strategist`
- `startup_operator`

Expected output:
- positioning
- messaging
- launch channels
- content ideas
- first acquisition plan

## Step 4 — Monetization
Skills:
- `startup_operator`

Expected output:
- pricing options
- revenue model
- tradeoffs

## Step 5 — Execution Plan
Skills:
- `technical_writer`

Expected output:
- 30/60/90 day plan
- milestones
- dependencies
- success metrics

## Rules
- prioritize validation over overbuilding
- focus on real traction
- keep MVP small
